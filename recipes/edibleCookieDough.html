Edible Cookie Dough


Classic Chocolate chip cookies, but better since its edible cookie dough.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar(packed)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup Milk Chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Mini Chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine brown sugar, milk and Butter, beat until creamy.
  2. beat in Vanilla and salt. Add Flour,until dough forms.
  3. Finally Fold in Chocolate chips.
