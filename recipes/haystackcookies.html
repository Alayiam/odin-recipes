Haystack Cookies


A quick and easy recipe with no baking required.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup Peanut Butter
  • 3 cups quick Oats
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan,Combine sugar, milk,butter, and cocoa.Bring to a boil, and cook for 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat, stir in Peanut butter, oata, and Vanilla.
  3. Drop teaspoonfuls onto wax paper, place in fridge and let harden.
  4. enjoy!
